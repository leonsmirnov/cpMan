Version 2.0 is a major update with new features, smarter editing, and a more polished experience throughout.

NEW FEATURES
- Image history: cpMan now captures screenshots and images you copy, not just text. Images are stored locally with optional size limits.
- Image search: text is automatically extracted from images (OCR) so you can search your image history by content.
- Auto-paste: select any item and cpMan pastes it directly into your active app, with no need to press Cmd+V manually. Toggle it in Settings > General.
- Pin items: pin any entry to keep it at the top of the list and exempt it from automatic cleanup.
- Private Mode: pause clipboard recording for 15 minutes, 1 hour, or indefinitely from the menu bar icon.
- Ignore list: add apps you never want recorded (for example, password managers) in Settings > Ignore List.
- History limits: set count, age, or size limits for how long history is kept in Settings > History.

IMPROVEMENTS
- Editing is now smooth and focused: cpMan stays in focus while you edit, and your edited text is pasted automatically on save. The original entry stays in its place in history.
- The Hotkeys screen now shows clear instructions and a "Reset to Default" button, so the shortcut is easy to change.
- Settings always reopens on the General tab.

FIXES
- Fixed an issue where a custom global shortcut could be lost after relaunching the app.
- Various stability and performance improvements.
